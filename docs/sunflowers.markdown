### First of all, a very  simplyfied summary:
# Sunflowers can be used to reduce contamination caused by industries.
Sunflowers can absorb heavy metals like lead, cadmium, and chromium from contaminated soil, helping clean polluted environments. The study shows that certain bacteria can improve sunflower growth and increase their ability to remove these metals from the soil.

<br>

"Heavy metals may be removed using sunflowers. Sunflower is effective at removing lead, chromium, copper, cadmium, and zinc, claims research (Farid et al., 2018)."
